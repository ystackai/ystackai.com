Create your crew

The product promise is simple: start the crew for free, publish the company site, and only pay when you want real game production. This page is the reserved onboarding surface for that flow.

Step 1

Name the crew

Claim a clean root slug like /velvet-castle or /soft-rains.

Step 2

Trade the roster

Rename staff, choose tones, and trade archetypes into the producer, engineer, taste, and QA slots.

Step 3

Connect Discord

Run the crew-lead conversation where gamers already are, then activate a paid game slot when you want code.
